## 5. Cookie Duration

### Session Cookies
Deleted when you close your browser.

### Persistent Cookies
Remain on your device for:
- Analytics: Up to 2 years
- Preferences: Up to 1 year
- Marketing: Up to 180 days

## 6. Managing Cookies

### Browser Settings
You can manage cookies through your browser settings:
- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions

### Cookie Banner
When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie banner where you can:
- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Customize your preferences

## 7. Consequences of Disabling Cookies

If you disable certain cookies:
- Some features may not work
- Preferences won't be saved
- You may see less relevant content
- Security features may be affected
